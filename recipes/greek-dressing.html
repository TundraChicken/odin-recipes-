Greek Salad Dressing

Description

This is an easy to make salad dressing that is better than store bought. The ingredients are simple things that are almost always on hand and no special equipment is required. We love putting this dressing on everything from salad to potatoes! This dressing stores well for a few days in the fridge, just remember to set it out at room temperature about an hour before using so it has time to soften.

Ingredients

  • 0.25 cups olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp red wine viengar
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 0.25 tsp dijon mustar
  • a pinch of oregano

Steps

  1. Measure out all ingredients into a glass jar or small bowl
  2. Mix well using a fork or by putting the lid on the glass jar and shaking
  3. Serve on top of your favorite salad or as a sauce for chicken and potatoes
