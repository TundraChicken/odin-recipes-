Taco Bowl

Description

One of our personal favorite recipes! Easy and cheap to make, this taco bowl will not dissapoint. The meat can be whatever you have on hand and the topppings can be customized to your liking. Adding in some fresh jalapenos and onion gives the bowl a good balance of textures. You can use cheese or avacado to give it a creamy finish!

Ingredients

  • 0.75 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder
  • 1 cup of cooked white rice
  • 1 avocado, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, chopped
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • hot sauce or other toppings

Steps

  1. Cook rice by your preffered method so it is ready when you are done with the beef. We personally love rice cookers!
  2. Cook ground beef in pan on med-high until just starting to crisp up
  3. SAdd in the salt and spices to the cooked beef and turn the pan to low. You may want to add a splash of whater to help coat all the beef with the spices
  4. Dish out the rice into bowls, put beef on top and top with avacado, onion and whatever else you'd like
  5. Stir for a well balanced bowl. Enjoy!
